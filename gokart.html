Safety First

Helmet
The first piece of safety equipment to protect anyone racing go-karts is the helmet
Gloves
Gloves are more of an accessory than an actual piece of go-kart safety equipment, but they can serve multiple purposes. For one, go-karting for younger kids can make them feel like real racecar drivers
Chest Protectors
Chest protectors are another essential piece of racing equipment that you need to have, especially if you are serious about racing.
